ORTHOPEDICS

XYZ Hospital Limited has one of best the orthopaedic department in the country, serving to provide 360º care as a Bone & Joint Centre. The Department of Orthopaedics has two full-time Consultants, Two part-time Consultant, a number of junior doctors, and supporting paramedical staff trained to deliver a 100% success rate. Our team diagnoses, operates and treats problems affecting the bones, joints and muscles of patients encompassing all age groups.

The Department of Orthopaedics offers out-patient consultation services and procedures. It is well supported by fully equipped operation theatre (OT), intensive care units for pre- and post- operative care, as well as rehabilitation services in Physiotherapy Department. The state-of-the-art OT is equipped all updated facilities. The hospital is also equipped with CT scan (500 slice), MRI Scan (1.5 tesla), and Digital X-ray for prompt screening facilities.
